About me

Computer nerd, Scuba diver, wanna-be coder. After graduating high school and joining the US Air Force, I spent four years doing the work that the military thought I should do.... autobody repair and painting! As alluring a life of automotive work may be for some, it wasn't for me.

I spent my free time selling items on eBay and trying to learn how to code my own e-commerce sites. After a few years of hacking my way through the weeds that is web development, I knew enough to be completely competent at breaking things.

Now, it's time to get serious. After looking at the options of going back to school for a degree or attending a bootcamp, I opted for the latter.

I am currently attending the full stack web development bootcamp at the University of Utah. Upon graduating in December 2017 I will be pursuing employment as an entry level web dev!
